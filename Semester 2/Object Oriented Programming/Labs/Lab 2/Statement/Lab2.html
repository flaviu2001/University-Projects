Details for Altarnative (sic) services

Solvable To Lab Number
4

A small set of basic incantations was handed to you by Huric on the night of your arrival – along with a stern recommendation to practice as often as you could, but to never prioritize it over more important requests. Tonight, you once again have to go through the usual motions. You place the Borhild gemstones in their corresponding sockets on the cold stonework of the altar. It hums and pulses with hidden energies. You take out the scroll given to you by your master, and consult the set of exercises – devising an incantation using the First School rune system has become almost second-nature. Presently, you have to weave a runic spell (in the old C language) that could animate your quill and have it automatically solve a series of calculations. Make sure that all spells(functions) are specified.

For a list of dates, determine the day of the year for each.
